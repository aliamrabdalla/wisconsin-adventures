BAILEYS HARBOR, WI

$40-$100 DAY HALF DAY OUTDOOR MODERATE SUMMER

TAKE THE CAVE POINT TOUR WITH DOOR COUNTY ADVENTURE RAFTING

Bonus: Have a picnic afterwards at Baileys Harbor Ridges County Park!

Explore Lake Michigan's dramatic sea caves by inflatable raft.

What to Do

  • Reserve the Cave Point Tour online — morning slots get calmer water
  • Meet at the launch site and get fitted with gear
  • Paddle through sea caves and along the rocky shoreline with your guide
  • After the tour, drive to Baileys Harbor Ridges County Park for a picnic
  • Hike the boardwalk through the ridges to stretch your legs

Good to Know

  • Tours run June through September depending on weather
  • Dress to get wet — bring a change of clothes
  • WI state park sticker may be required for nearby parks
