WI-003

BARABOO, WI

$0-$15 DAY HALF DAY OUTDOOR ACTIVE SPR-FALL

HIKE THE BALANCED ROCK TRAIL AT DEVIL'S LAKE STATE PARK

Bonus: Spend time swimming and having a picnic at the beach!

Tackle one of Wisconsin's best bluff hikes with incredible lake views.

What to Do

  • Arrive early to snag parking at the north shore lot
  • Start on the East Bluff Trail and hike up to Balanced Rock
  • Continue along the bluff for panoramic views of the lake
  • Loop back down via the CCC Trail or Grottos Trail
  • Cool off with a swim at the north shore beach

Good to Know

  • $13 daily vehicle pass or annual WI state park sticker required
  • The bluff trails have steep rocky sections — wear sturdy shoes
  • Weekends are packed in summer — arrive before 9 AM
