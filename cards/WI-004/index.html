BAYFIELD, WI

$40-$100 DAY FULL DAY OUTDOOR ACTIVE SUMMER

KAYAK THE APOSTLE ISLANDS SEA CAVES

Bonus: Explore downtown Bayfield and grab fresh whitefish at a local spot!

Paddle through carved sandstone sea caves on Lake Superior.

What to Do

  • Book a guided sea cave kayak tour with a Bayfield outfitter
  • Check the weather — tours require calm lake conditions
  • Gear up and paddle out to the mainland sea caves
  • Explore sandstone arches, chambers, and colorful rock faces
  • After the tour, walk the Bayfield waterfront and grab lunch

Good to Know

  • Book well in advance — summer tours sell out fast
  • Tours depend on Lake Superior conditions and may cancel
  • Plan for a full day — the drive to Bayfield is part of the adventure
