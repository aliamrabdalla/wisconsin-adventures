WI-005

CABLE, WI

$0-$15 DAY 2-4H OUTDOOR ACTIVE SPR-FALL

MOUNTAIN BIKE THE CAMBA TRAILS TOGETHER

Bonus: Reward yourselves with a burger and a beer in Cable afterwards!

Ride world-class singletrack through the Chequamegon National Forest.

What to Do

  • Rent mountain bikes in Cable if you don't have your own
  • Start with the Rock Lake or Short & Fat trail for intermediate riding
  • Ride through hardwood forests, rolling hills, and lakeside terrain
  • Take a break at a scenic overlook and refuel with snacks
  • Clean up and head into town for a post-ride meal
