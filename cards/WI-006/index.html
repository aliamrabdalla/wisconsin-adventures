CEDARBURG, WI

$15-$40 EITHER 2-4H BOTH CHILL ANY

EXPLORE HISTORIC DOWNTOWN CEDARBURG

Bonus: Stop at the Cedar Creek Winery in the old woolen mill!

Wander a charming small-town Main Street full of shops, art, and wine.

What to Do

  • Start on Washington Avenue and browse independent shops and galleries
  • Visit the Cedar Creek Settlement — a restored 1864 woolen mill with a winery
  • Do a wine tasting at Cedar Creek Winery
  • Walk to the last covered bridge in Wisconsin at Covered Bridge Park
  • Grab lunch or coffee at one of the downtown cafes

Good to Know

  • Free street parking — fills up on festival weekends
  • The town hosts seasonal festivals — check the event calendar
  • Most shops open 10-5 daily
