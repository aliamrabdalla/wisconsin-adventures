CHIPPEWA FALLS, WI

$0-$15 DAY 1-2H INDOOR CHILL ANY

TAKE THE LEINENKUGEL'S BREWERY TOUR

Bonus: Walk the Chippewa River State Trail along the water afterwards!

Tour one of America's oldest breweries and sample the lineup.

What to Do

  • Head to the Leinenkugel's Brewery & Leinie Lodge in Chippewa Falls
  • Sign up for the brewery tour — walk-ins welcome, check seasonal hours
  • Tour the brewing facility and learn the 150+ year history
  • Enjoy free samples in the tasting room

Good to Know

  • Tours are free — samples included
  • Must be 21+ for tastings with valid ID
  • Pair with a walk on the Chippewa River State Trail nearby
