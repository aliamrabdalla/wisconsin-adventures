$40-$100 DAY 2-4H OUTDOOR CHILL SUMMER

RENT A PONTOON AND CRUISE THE EAGLE RIVER CHAIN OF LAKES

Bonus: Pack a cooler and anchor at a sandy beach for a swim break!

Cruise the world's largest chain of inland lakes on your own pontoon.

What to Do

  • Reserve a pontoon rental from a local marina in Eagle River
  • Get a quick orientation and pick up a lake map
  • Cruise through the 28-lake chain connected by channels and rivers
  • Anchor at a sandy beach or island for a swim break
  • Spot eagles, loons, and herons along the way
