WI-009

EAU CLAIRE, WI

$0-$15 DAY 2-4H OUTDOOR CHILL SUMMER

FLOAT THE CHIPPEWA RIVER ON INNER TUBES

Bonus: Grab tacos and margaritas at a downtown spot after your float!

Lazy-river tubing through town — bring snacks and drift.

What to Do

  • Rent tubes from a local outfitter near Phoenix Park
  • Launch at the upstream put-in and float the Chippewa River
  • Drift through the middle of town for 1-2 hours
  • Exit at the designated take-out point
  • Walk to a downtown restaurant for food and drinks

Good to Know

  • Tubing depends on weather and water levels
  • Wear water shoes — some sections have rocky bottoms
  • Best from June through August
