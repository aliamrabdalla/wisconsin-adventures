FOND DU LAC, WI

$0-$15 DAY 1-2H OUTDOOR CHILL SPR-FALL

STROLL LAKESIDE PARK AND VISIT THE LIGHTHOUSE

Bonus: Rent a paddleboat on the lagoon!

Walk through a lakeside park with a lighthouse, gardens, and a lagoon.

What to Do

  • Park at Lakeside Park along Lake Winnebago
  • Walk out to the historic lighthouse at the end of the pier
  • Stroll through the flower gardens and along the paved paths
  • Rent a paddleboat on the lagoon if weather allows
  • Grab ice cream from a nearby spot and enjoy it in the park
