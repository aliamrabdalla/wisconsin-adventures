GALESVILLE, WI

$0-$15 DAY 2-4H OUTDOOR MODERATE SPR-FALL

HIKE THE TREMPEALEAU NATIONAL WILDLIFE REFUGE

Bonus: Stop at the historic Trempealeau Hotel for a walleye dinner!

Walk through marshlands and bluffs along the Mississippi River.

What to Do

  • Drive to the Trempealeau National Wildlife Refuge visitor area
  • Pick up a trail map and start on the Prairie or Marsh Trail
  • Watch for bald eagles, herons, and other wildlife along the river
  • Drive the 4.5-mile wildlife auto tour loop for more scenery
  • Head to nearby Trempealeau for dinner at the historic hotel

Good to Know

  • The refuge is free and open dawn to dusk
  • Bring binoculars for birding
