TAKE A LAMBEAU FIELD STADIUM TOUR

Go behind the scenes at the legendary home of the Green Bay Packers.

What to Do

  • Book the Lambeau Field Stadium Tour online or at the Packers Pro Shop
  • Walk through the player tunnel onto the field
  • Visit the luxury suites, press box, and Packers Hall of Fame
  • Browse the Packers Pro Shop for gear
  • Grab lunch at one of the restaurants inside the Lambeau Atrium

Good to Know

  • Tours run year-round but schedules vary on game days
  • The Hall of Fame is included with the tour or available separately
  • Allow 60-90 minutes for the full tour
