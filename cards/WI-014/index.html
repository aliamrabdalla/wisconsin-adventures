WI-014

HAYWARD, WI

$15-$40 DAY 2-4H OUTDOOR CHILL SUMMER

VISIT WILDERNESS WALK ZOO AND EXPLORE DOWNTOWN HAYWARD

Bonus: Grab a giant scoop at West's Hayward Dairy on your way out!

Walk among free-roaming deer and native animals in a northwoods zoo.

What to Do

  • Head to Wilderness Walk on the south end of Hayward
  • Walk the wooded paths and feed the free-roaming deer and animals
  • Check out the baby animal nursery if visiting in early summer
  • Drive into downtown Hayward and take a photo at the National Fresh Water Fishing Hall of Fame muskie
  • Cool down with ice cream at a local dairy shop

Good to Know

  • Open seasonally — typically mid-May through Labor Day
  • Bring quarters for the animal feed dispensers
  • Great for a relaxed afternoon — plan about 2 hours
