HUBERTUS, WI

$0-$15 DAY 1-2H OUTDOOR MODERATE ANY

HIKE TO THE SCENIC TOWER AT HOLY HILL

Bonus: Visit the gift shop and grab a bag of monastery caramels!

Climb the scenic tower at a hilltop basilica for panoramic views of the Kettle Moraine.

What to Do

  • Drive to the Basilica of the National Shrine of Mary at Holy Hill
  • Climb the scenic tower for 360-degree views of the Kettle Moraine
  • Walk the grounds and the outdoor Stations of the Cross trail
  • Visit the gift shop inside the basilica

Good to Know

  • The scenic tower requires a small fee — grounds are free
  • Fall colors from the tower are spectacular (late September-October)
  • Dress respectfully for the basilica — it's an active church
