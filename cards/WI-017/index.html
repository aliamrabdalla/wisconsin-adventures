KOHLER, WI

$0-$15 DAY 2-4H BOTH CHILL ANY

TOUR THE KOHLER DESIGN CENTER AND EXPLORE THE VILLAGE

Bonus: Treat yourselves to lunch at one of the Kohler restaurants!

See stunning kitchen and bath designs in a free museum-like showroom.

What to Do

  • Start at the Kohler Design Center on Highland Drive
  • Browse three floors of kitchen, bath, and design exhibits
  • Check out the art gallery and Kohler history displays
  • Walk through the manicured Kohler village streets
  • Stop for a nice lunch at one of the village restaurants

Good to Know

  • The Design Center is free and open Monday-Saturday
  • The village of Kohler has a planned-community charm worth walking
  • Pair with a trip to Kohler-Andrae State Park 15 minutes east
