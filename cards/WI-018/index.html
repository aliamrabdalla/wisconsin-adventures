WI-018

LA CROSSE, WI

$0-$15 DAY 1-2H OUTDOOR MODERATE SPR-FALL

HIKE GRANDAD BLUFF FOR A SUNSET VIEW

Bonus: Grab dinner at a restaurant on the river after the hike!

Climb to the top of Grandad Bluff for sweeping views of the Mississippi River valley.

What to Do

  • Drive up to Grandad Bluff Park (you can also hike from the base)
  • Walk to the main overlook for a panoramic view of three states
  • Hike the bluff trails if you want more exploration
  • Time it for golden hour if you can — the sunset is incredible
  • Head downtown to a Mississippi River restaurant for dinner

Good to Know

  • Free parking at the top — the road is steep but paved
  • The overlook gets crowded at sunset on weekends
  • Bring layers — it's windy at the top
