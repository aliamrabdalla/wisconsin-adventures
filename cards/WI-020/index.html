VISIT THE OLBRICH BOTANICAL GARDENS

Bonus: Grab Thai food on Atwood Avenue after your visit!

Wander 16 acres of outdoor gardens and a tropical conservatory.

What to Do

  • Head to Olbrich Botanical Gardens on the east side of Madison
  • Walk through the outdoor gardens — the rose garden and rock garden are highlights
  • Step inside the Bolz Conservatory for a tropical escape (small fee)
  • Sit by the lakefront garden area and enjoy the view of Lake Monona

Good to Know

  • Outdoor gardens are free — the conservatory has a small admission fee
  • The conservatory is open year-round (great winter date)
  • Holiday light show in December
