MADISON, WI

$15-$40 DAY 2-4H OUTDOOR CHILL SPR-FALL

EXPLORE THE DANE COUNTY FARMERS' MARKET ON THE CAPITOL SQUARE

Bonus: Take a free tour of the Wisconsin State Capitol building!

Browse the largest producer-only farmers' market in the country.

What to Do

  • Head to Capitol Square on a Saturday morning (market runs April-November)
  • Walk the full loop around the Capitol — grab cheese curds, pastries, and flowers
  • Pick up fresh produce, bread, and a bouquet
  • Take a free tour of the State Capitol building (offered on the hour)
  • Grab brunch at a nearby restaurant on the square

Good to Know

  • Saturday market runs 6:15 AM-1:45 PM, April through November
  • Arrive early for the best selection and easier parking
  • Bring cash — not all vendors accept cards
