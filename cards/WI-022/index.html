WALK TO PICNIC POINT AND HANG OUT AT THE MEMORIAL UNION TERRACE

Bonus: Get Babcock Hall ice cream from the UW Dairy Store!

A lakeside walk to a secluded peninsula followed by Terrace hangs.

What to Do

  • Park near the Lakeshore Nature Preserve and walk out to Picnic Point
  • Follow the 0.7-mile path through woods to the tip of the peninsula
  • Find a fire pit or bench and enjoy the views of Lake Mendota
  • Walk back and head to the Memorial Union Terrace
  • Grab Babcock Hall ice cream or a pitcher and watch the sunset
