BROWSE THE CHAZEN MUSEUM OF ART AND WALK STATE STREET

Bonus: Try a different cuisine at one of State Street's many restaurants!

Free world-class art museum followed by a walk down Madison's iconic street.

What to Do

  • Start at the Chazen Museum of Art on the UW campus — admission is free
  • Explore the permanent collection spanning centuries and cultures
  • Check for rotating exhibits and any evening events
  • Walk down State Street, browsing bookshops and boutiques
  • Pick a restaurant for dinner — the options span every cuisine

Good to Know

  • The Chazen is free and open year-round (closed Mondays)
  • State Street is pedestrian-only between the Capitol and campus
  • Evenings on State Street are lively — restaurants, bars, and live music
