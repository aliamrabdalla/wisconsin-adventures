TOUR THE SUBMARINE AT THE WISCONSIN MARITIME MUSEUM

Bonus: Walk the Manitowoc Riverwalk along the harbor afterwards!

Explore a real WWII submarine and Lake Michigan maritime history.

What to Do

  • Head to the Wisconsin Maritime Museum in downtown Manitowoc
  • Tour the USS Cobia — a WWII submarine docked right outside
  • Explore the museum exhibits on Great Lakes shipping and shipbuilding
  • Walk the Manitowoc Riverwalk along the harbor

Good to Know

  • Submarine tour is included with museum admission
  • The submarine has tight spaces — not for the claustrophobic
  • Allow about 2 hours for the museum and sub tour
