MERRIMAC, WI

$0-$15 DAY 2-4H OUTDOOR MODERATE SPR-FALL

RIDE THE FREE MERRIMAC FERRY AND HIKE PARFREY'S GLEN

Bonus: Stop for a burger and a shake in Merrimac after your hike!

Take a free car ferry across the Wisconsin River, then hike a hidden canyon.

What to Do

  • Drive to the Merrimac Ferry landing and ride across the Wisconsin River (free!)
  • Drive to Parfrey's Glen State Natural Area (15 min from the ferry)
  • Hike the 1.5-mile trail into the glen — a narrow canyon with waterfalls
  • Wade through the creek crossings together (wear shoes that can get wet)
  • Take the ferry back and grab food in Merrimac

Good to Know

  • The ferry runs April through November, dawn to dusk
  • Parfrey's Glen trail is rocky and wet — wear sturdy waterproof shoes
  • State park pass required for the glen parking lot
