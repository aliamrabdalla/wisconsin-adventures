EXPLORE THE HISTORIC THIRD WARD AND MILWAUKEE PUBLIC MARKET

Bonus: Catch a show at the Milwaukee Rep or Pabst Theater!

Browse the Public Market, boutique shops, and galleries in Milwaukee's coolest neighborhood.

What to Do

  • Start at the Milwaukee Public Market — grab a snack or coffee
  • Browse the specialty food vendors and pick up treats to share
  • Walk through the Third Ward's art galleries and boutique shops
  • Stroll along the Milwaukee Riverwalk
  • Grab dinner or drinks at one of the neighborhood's restaurants

Good to Know

  • The Public Market is open daily — weekday mornings are less crowded
  • Street parking and nearby garages available
  • The neighborhood hosts summer festivals and gallery nights
