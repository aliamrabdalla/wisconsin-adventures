TAKE THE LAKEFRONT BREWERY TOUR

Bonus: Walk the lakefront trail along Lake Michigan after the tour!

The most entertaining brewery tour in Wisconsin — with polka.

What to Do

  • Book the Lakefront Brewery tour online (they sell out)
  • Show up and enjoy the high-energy, comedy-filled tour
  • Sample four beers included with your tour ticket
  • Browse the merch shop and grab a souvenir glass
  • Walk the lakefront trail or explore the nearby Beerline Trail

Good to Know

  • Book online — Friday and Saturday tours sell out fast
  • Must be 21+ with valid ID
  • The tour is known for being fun and entertaining, not stuffy
