DO A GALLERY WALK AND VISIT A CRAFT BREWERY IN MINERAL POINT

Bonus: Check if a live glass-blowing demo is happening at Brewery Pottery!

Browse artist studios, galleries, and a brewery in Wisconsin's most artistic small town.

What to Do

  • Start on High Street and wander through the artist galleries and studios
  • Watch artists at work — many studios are open to visitors
  • Visit Pendarvis, the restored Cornish miners' village (seasonal)
  • Head to a local brewery for a craft beer and a bite

Good to Know

  • Gallery Night events happen several times a year — check the calendar
  • Many studios close on weekdays — weekends are best for visiting
  • The town has deep Cornish mining history worth exploring
