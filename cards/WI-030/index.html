WI-030

MINOCQUA, WI

$15-$40 DAY 2-4H OUTDOOR CHILL SUMMER

SPEND THE DAY AT WILDWOOD WILDLIFE PARK AND ZOO

Bonus: Rent a kayak or canoe on Lake Minocqua afterwards!

Feed giraffes, ride camels, and meet hundreds of animals at a northwoods zoo.

What to Do

  • Head to Wildwood Wildlife Park & Nature Center in Minocqua
  • Walk through the park and interact with the animals — many are feed-friendly
  • Check the schedule for feeding times and animal encounters
  • Take the safari ride if available
  • Cool off with a swim or kayak rental on Lake Minocqua

Good to Know

  • Open seasonally — typically mid-May through late September
  • Bring cash for animal food and extras
  • Plan 2-3 hours for the full park
