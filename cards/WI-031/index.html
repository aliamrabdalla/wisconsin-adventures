WI-031

MOUNT HOREB, WI

$15-$40 DAY 2-4H BOTH CHILL ANY

EXPLORE CAVE OF THE MOUNDS AND THE TROLLWAY

Bonus: Walk through downtown Mount Horeb and spot all the troll sculptures!

Tour a dazzling underground cave and walk a troll-themed trail.

What to Do

  • Drive to Cave of the Mounds west of Mount Horeb
  • Take the guided cave tour through the limestone formations
  • Explore the aboveground gardens and rock displays
  • Head into Mount Horeb and walk the Trollway — spot all the troll sculptures
  • Browse the Scandinavian-themed shops on Main Street

Good to Know

  • The cave is a constant 50°F — bring a light jacket
  • Open year-round — a great winter date option
  • Allow about 1 hour for the cave tour
