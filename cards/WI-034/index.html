WI-034

PEPIN, WI

$15-$40 DAY 2-4H BOTH CHILL SPR-FALL

VISIT PEPIN VILLAGE AND GET PIE IN STOCKHOLM

Bonus: Drive the Great River Road scenic route along the Mississippi!

Browse a tiny river town and eat some of Wisconsin's best pie.

What to Do

  • Drive to Pepin on the Mississippi River via the Great River Road
  • Walk the small downtown — browse the shops and the Pepin Historical Museum
  • Drive 3 miles south to Stockholm and visit the Stockholm Pie & General Store
  • Order a slice (or a whole pie) and enjoy it with a river view
  • Drive the Great River Road north or south for scenic bluff views

Good to Know

  • Stockholm Pie is seasonal — check hours before visiting
  • Pepin is the birthplace of Laura Ingalls Wilder
  • The Great River Road is especially beautiful in fall
