WI-035

POTOSI, WI

$0-$15 DAY 1-2H INDOOR CHILL ANY

TOUR THE NATIONAL BREWERY MUSEUM AND POTOSI BREWING

Bonus: Drive the Great River Road and stop at the Potosi overlook!

Explore America's brewing history and taste local brews in a tiny river town.

What to Do

  • Head to the National Brewery Museum in Potosi
  • Tour the museum — it covers the full history of American brewing
  • Sample beers at the Potosi Brewing Company taproom next door
  • Check out the transportation museum on the lower level
  • Drive to a Great River Road overlook for Mississippi views

Good to Know

  • Museum admission is affordable — check for combo deals with tastings
  • Potosi is remote — plan it as part of a scenic drive day
  • The restaurant serves solid pub food
