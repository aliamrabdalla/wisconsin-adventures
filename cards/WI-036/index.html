WI-036

PRAIRIE DU CHIEN, WI

$0-$15 DAY 2-4H OUTDOOR MODERATE SPR-FALL

HIKE WYALUSING STATE PARK AND SEE WHERE THE RIVERS MEET

Bonus: Visit the Villa Louis historic mansion on St. Feriole Island!

Stand at the confluence of the Mississippi and Wisconsin Rivers from high bluffs.

What to Do

  • Drive to Wyalusing State Park south of Prairie du Chien
  • Head to Point Lookout for the iconic view of the two rivers meeting
  • Hike the Sentinel Ridge Trail along the bluff for more viewpoints
  • Explore the Indian Mounds trail — the park has effigy mounds
  • Drive into Prairie du Chien and visit Villa Louis if time allows

Good to Know

  • State park vehicle pass required ($13/day or annual)
  • The bluffs are spectacular in fall color season
  • Some trails are steep — wear sturdy shoes
