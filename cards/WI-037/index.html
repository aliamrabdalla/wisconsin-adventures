WI-037

SHEBOYGAN, WI

$0-$15 DAY 2-4H OUTDOOR MODERATE SPR-FALL

WALK THE DUNES AT KOHLER-ANDRAE STATE PARK

Bonus: Grab a Sheboygan bratwurst on the way home — it's the Bratwurst Capital!

Walk along Lake Michigan beaches and through rare sand dunes.

What to Do

  • Drive to Kohler-Andrae State Park south of Sheboygan
  • Walk the Cordwalk Nature Trail through the dunes and wetlands
  • Spend time on the beach — it's one of the best on Lake Michigan
  • Hike the Woodland Dunes Nature Trail for more exploring
  • Stop for a bratwurst in Sheboygan on the way out

Good to Know

  • State park vehicle pass required ($13/day or annual)
  • The beach sand dunes are fragile — stay on marked trails
  • Lake Michigan water is cold even in summer — but refreshing
