WI-038

SISTER BAY, WI

$15-$40 DAY 2-4H BOTH MODERATE SPR-FALL

EAT AT AL JOHNSON'S (GOATS ON THE ROOF!) AND BIKE DOOR COUNTY

Bonus: Grab a cherry pie or cherry wine from a Door County orchard!

Have Swedish pancakes under a goat-covered roof, then bike through Door County.

What to Do

  • Head to Al Johnson's Swedish Restaurant in Sister Bay
  • Watch the goats grazing on the sod roof while you wait
  • Order the Swedish pancakes with lingonberries — it's the signature dish
  • Rent bikes nearby and ride through the scenic countryside
  • Stop at a cherry orchard or winery along the way

Good to Know

  • Al Johnson's is cash-only (or check) — no credit cards
  • Expect a wait on summer weekends — it's worth it
  • The goats are on the roof from spring through fall
