GET LOST IN THE HOUSE ON THE ROCK

Bonus: Grab dinner at the Shed in Spring Green after your visit!

Explore one of Wisconsin's most bizarre and fascinating attractions.

What to Do

  • Drive to House on the Rock south of Spring Green
  • Choose how many sections to explore — there are three to walk through
  • Marvel at the Infinity Room, the world's largest carousel, and the bizarre collections
  • Take your time — there's no rushing through this place
  • Stop in Spring Green for dinner afterwards

Good to Know

  • Allow 3-4 hours if you want to see all three sections
  • Tickets are sold by section — Section 1 is the must-see
  • The Infinity Room extends 218 feet over the valley — not for those afraid of heights
