WI-041

STEVENS POINT, WI

$0-$15 DAY 2-4H OUTDOOR MODERATE SPR-FALL

BIKE OR HIKE THE GREEN CIRCLE TRAIL

Bonus: Tour the Stevens Point Brewery (one of Wisconsin's oldest) afterwards!

Follow a 26-mile loop trail through woods, rivers, and prairies.

What to Do

  • Park at Pfiffner Pioneer Park or another trailhead along the Green Circle
  • Hike or bike a section of the 26-mile loop through varied terrain
  • Cross the Wisconsin River and enjoy the riverside stretches
  • Pick a section that matches your energy — some are paved, some are woodsy
  • End at a local restaurant or the Stevens Point Brewery for refreshments

Good to Know

  • The trail is free and open year-round
  • Some segments are unpaved — check conditions if biking
  • The Standing Rocks segment has great geological features
