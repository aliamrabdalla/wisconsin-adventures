STOCKHOLM, WI

$15-$40 DAY HALF DAY BOTH CHILL SPR-FALL

DRIVE THE GREAT RIVER ROAD AND EXPLORE TINY STOCKHOLM

Bonus: Stop at every scenic overlook and take photos of the Mississippi bluffs!

Cruise one of America's most scenic drives and explore a village of 66 people.

What to Do

  • Start your Great River Road drive from wherever you're coming from
  • Stop at scenic overlooks along the Mississippi River bluffs
  • Pull into Stockholm — population 66 — and browse the art galleries
  • Visit the Stockholm Pie & General Store for homemade pie
  • Continue your drive north or south along the river

Good to Know

  • Stockholm shops and bakeries are seasonal — check hours
  • The drive is gorgeous in fall color season (late September-October)
  • Combine with Pepin (3 miles north) for a full day trip
