PADDLE THE YAHARA RIVER TOGETHER

Bonus: Explore downtown Stoughton's Norwegian-themed Main Street!

Canoe or kayak a gentle river through farmland and wetlands.

What to Do

  • Rent canoes or kayaks from a local outfitter in Stoughton
  • Launch on the Yahara River and paddle downstream
  • Float through wetlands, farmland, and gentle stretches
  • Watch for herons, turtles, and other wildlife along the banks
  • Walk downtown Stoughton's Main Street for a Norwegian-themed vibe

Good to Know

  • The river is calm and beginner-friendly
