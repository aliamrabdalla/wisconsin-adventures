STURGEON BAY, WI

$40-$100 DAY HALF DAY BOTH CHILL SPR-FALL

HIT THE DOOR COUNTY WINE TRAIL

Bonus: End the day with a fish boil at a Door County restaurant!

Sip your way through scenic Door County wineries.

What to Do

  • Start in Sturgeon Bay and pick 2-3 wineries from the Door County Wine Trail
  • Do tastings at each stop — each winery has its own vibe and specialties
  • Grab cheese and crackers to share between stops
  • Enjoy the vineyard scenery and waterfront locations
  • End the day with a traditional Door County fish boil for dinner

Good to Know

  • Designate a driver or use a tour service
  • Most wineries are open spring through fall — check individual hours
  • Fish boils are a Door County tradition — look for the outdoor boilover show
