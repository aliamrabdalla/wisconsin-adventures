WI-045

SUPERIOR, WI

$0-$15 DAY 1-2H OUTDOOR MODERATE SPR-FALL

HIKE TO BIG MANITOU FALLS — WISCONSIN'S TALLEST WATERFALL

Bonus: Drive across the Bong Bridge to Duluth for dinner with a harbor view!

See Wisconsin's highest waterfall plunge 165 feet through a gorge.

What to Do

  • Drive to Pattison State Park south of Superior
  • Follow the short trail to the Big Manitou Falls overlook
  • Take in the 165-foot waterfall — the tallest in Wisconsin
  • Hike the trail to Little Manitou Falls for a second waterfall
  • If time allows, cross into Duluth, MN for dinner

Good to Know

  • State park vehicle pass required ($13/day or annual)
  • The falls are most impressive in spring and after rain
  • The trail to the falls is short but has stairs
