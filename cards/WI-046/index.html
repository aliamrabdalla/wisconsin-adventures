CANOE THE NAMEKAGON RIVER THROUGH THE NATIONAL SCENIC RIVERWAY

Bonus: Pack a picnic and pull over at a sandbar for a riverside lunch!

Paddle a wild, pristine river through the St. Croix National Scenic Riverway.

What to Do

  • Book a canoe or kayak rental with shuttle service from a Trego outfitter
  • Get dropped off upstream and paddle back to the outfitter
  • Drift through old-growth forest and sandy banks
  • Pull over at a sandbar for a swim or picnic break
  • Watch for bald eagles, otters, and turtles along the way

Good to Know

  • The river is calm and great for beginners
  • Bring waterproof bags for phones and snacks
  • Book trips in advance for summer weekends
