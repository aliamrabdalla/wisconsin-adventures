Walk miles of Lake Michigan shoreline through dunes and forest.

What to Do

  • Drive to Point Beach State Forest north of Two Rivers
  • Start with the Rawley Point Trail along the Lake Michigan shore
  • Walk the beach and hunt for beach glass and interesting rocks
  • Visit the Rawley Point Lighthouse (exterior viewing)
  • Hike the Red Pine Trail through the interior forest

Good to Know

  • State park vehicle pass required ($13/day or annual)
  • The beach is long and uncrowded even in summer
  • The lighthouse is photogenic but not open for interior tours
