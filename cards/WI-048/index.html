WI-048

WAUSAU, WI

$0-$15 DAY 1-2H OUTDOOR MODERATE ANY

HIKE RIB MOUNTAIN FOR PANORAMIC VIEWS

Bonus: Grab hot cocoa or a meal at a Wausau restaurant after the hike!

Hike one of the highest points in Wisconsin for sweeping views of the countryside.

What to Do

  • Drive to Rib Mountain State Park on the west side of Wausau
  • Hike the trail to the observation tower at the summit
  • Take in the panoramic views of the surrounding farmland and forests
  • Explore the other hiking trails if you want a longer outing
  • Head into Wausau for food and drinks

Good to Know

  • State park vehicle pass required ($13/day or annual)
  • In winter, Granite Peak ski area operates on the mountain
