WI-050

WISCONSIN DELLS, WI

$15-$40 DAY 1-2H OUTDOOR CHILL SPR-FALL

TAKE THE LOST CANYON HORSE-DRAWN CARRIAGE TOUR

Bonus: Hike the Witches Gulch trail nearby for more canyon views!

Ride a horse-drawn carriage through the deepest and longest land canyon in Wisconsin.

What to Do

  • Head to Lost Canyon Tours on Canyon Road in the Dells
  • Board a horse-drawn carriage for the guided canyon tour
  • Ride through towering sandstone walls up to 80 feet high
  • Enjoy the narrated history and geology of the canyon
  • After the tour, check out the Witches Gulch trail for more sandstone scenery

Good to Know

  • Tours run spring through fall — check seasonal hours
  • The ride takes about 30-45 minutes
  • Witches Gulch is accessible by Upper Dells Boat Tour — check availability
